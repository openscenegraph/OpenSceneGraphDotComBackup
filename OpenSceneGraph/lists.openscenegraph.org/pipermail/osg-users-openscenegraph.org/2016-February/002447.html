> starting with #pragma. When a line is found it looks for the next opening

> But when the #pragma line does not have a opening bracket but some
> following line has the analyzed text is wrong.
>

>

> varying vec3 atmos_vert;

> varying vec3 atmos_vert;
> void atmos_fragment_main" which looks wrong to me.
