Hi Tom,
Your model works with texture for me, in osg 3.4.0 as well as 3.6.5 with
the following commands:
sed -E "s#(f [0-9]+/[0-9]+)/0 ([0-9]+/[0-9]+)/0 ([0-9]+/[0-9]+)/0#\1 \2
\3#" "Data2.obj" > noNormals.obj
osgviewer -O noRotation  noNormals.obj
Laurens.
