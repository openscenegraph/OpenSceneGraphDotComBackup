Hi Etienne,

To toggle on/off a Camera the most efficient way to do it is to toggle
it's NodeMask i.e

  // switch off
  slave_camera->setNodeMask(0x0);

 // switch on
 slave_camera->setNodeMask(0xffffffff);

Robert.
