Yes. In FindCOLLADA.cmake :

         IF (COLLADA_INCLUDE_DOMANY_DIR)
             SET(COLLADA_DOM_2_4_OR_LATER TRUE)
         ELSEIF()
             SET(COLLADA_DOM_2_4_OR_LATER FALSE)
         ENDIF()

You will have to unset/remove entry COLLADA_INCLUDE_DOMANY_DIR .
