Hi David,

to set up a Quat with your hpr you can do something like this:
osg::Quat q;
q.makeRotate(h,osg::Vec3(0,0,1),p,osg::Vec3(1,0,0),r,osg::Vec3(0,1,0));

of course you can tune the order
