Hi, Yu,
    You can manually use osgconv.exe to convert between model files.  Run "osgconv.exe --help" for help.
    Also, u can use osgDB::readNodeFile() and osgDB::writeNodeFile() respectively with filenames with proper extension, such as ".osg" and ".osgb".
