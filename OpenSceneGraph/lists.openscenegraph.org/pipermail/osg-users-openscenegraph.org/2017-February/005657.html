Hi Kevin,
you solved the build problem, so never mind the cmake file.
the libsfxsdk.dylib needs to be in your library path, as it's needed to
load osgdb_fbx.so
A quick search suggests you could add "/Applications/Autodesk/FBX
SDK/2017.1/lib/clang/release/libfbxsdk.dylib" to DYLD_LIBRARY_PATH or get
the path into
osgdb_fbx with the linker option "-install_name" .
Laurens.
