On 9 February 2017 at 03:01, 韩志平 <hanzhiping at siasun.com> wrote:
> I want to use the file of .wrl.  How to get osgdb_vrml.dll?  The address to openscenegraph-third-party-library-downloads?

Links to the downloads we provide are on the openscenegraph website,
just look under downloads then dependencies.

However, the vrml plugin depends upon OpenVRML which is not a commonly
used format and dependency these days so it's unlikely to be included
in third party downloads.  You will mostly likely need to just build
OpenVRML from source.
