> So, isn't possible to have both HORIZONTAL and VERTICAL?
Keeping horizontal and vertical effectively is the FIXED policy. You 
handle the resize yourself if you need to apply something else.
The default resize handler tries to keep the aspect in a way things 
don't get distorted. The only thing distorted in your example is the 
text, which somehow is not reacting to resize in a sane manner.
