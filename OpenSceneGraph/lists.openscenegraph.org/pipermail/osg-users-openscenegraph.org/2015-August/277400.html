A big day for the OSG starts today.  First up the OpenSceneGraph-3.2.3
maintenance release.  It's just a small bump from 3.2.2, this time includes
revised volumer shaders to workaround an NVidia driver bug and a fix to an
error in the zip plugins handling of memory buffers.
