Hi Baka,

You need to pass in a model to the view to see anything i.e.

   osgViewer::Viewer viewer;
   viewer.setSceneData(osgDB::readNodeFile("cow.osgt");
   return viewer.run();

Please have a look at the various OSG examples in OpenSceneGraph/examples.
