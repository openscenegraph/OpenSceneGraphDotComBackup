Hi Chritoph,

The right way to do is to to assign an osg::Uniform to each
osg::View's master Camera's StateSet i,e


  view->getCamera()->getOrCreateStateSet()->addUniform(new
osg::Uniform("viewport", osg:::Vec4(x,y,w,h));

Robert
