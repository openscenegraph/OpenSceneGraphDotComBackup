Hi,

I found the solution on my own. For anyone who is interested in the topic, here is the solution:

The problem was that i checked the option "BUILD_OPENTHREADS_WITH_QT" in the cmake options. If that option is not set active and everything is recompiled, the error does not appear anymore.
