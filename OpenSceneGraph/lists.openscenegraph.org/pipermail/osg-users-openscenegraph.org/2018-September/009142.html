Robert,
Discourse (https://www.discourse.org) is likely the platform I would choose
today for osgEarth if starting from scratch. It is founded by Jeff Atwood
(of StackOverflow fame), it's open source so you can host it yourself, or
you can pay $100/month for them to host it for you.

Apparently they will sometimes offer discounts or even free hosting for
other open source projects:
https://blog.discourse.org/2016/03/free-discourse-forum-hosting-for-community-friendly-github-projects/
