The links I've sent have vegetation cover, which can be used as a texture. 
If you need photographic images, you can look for Landsat or other 
satellite images (I have downloaded some from Brazil only, maybe India have 
a similar service, look at your space agency), or even georeference a 
Google Earth printscreen, which can be done in QGIS, for instance.
