Hi Erik,

That looks like a texture wrap issue. Try setting your texture wrap mode to
CLAMP_TO_EDGE:

pTexture->setWrap(osg::Texture::WRAP_S, osg::Texture::CLAMP_TO_EDGE);
pTexture->setWrap(osg::Texture::WRAP_T, osg::Texture::CLAMP_TO_EDGE);

Cheers,
Farshid
