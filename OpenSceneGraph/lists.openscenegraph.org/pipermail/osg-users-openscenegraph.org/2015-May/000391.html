Hi Matt,

Use a combination of custom NodeVisitor to traverse the scene, and an
osg::Drawable::ArrayFunctor to traverse the vertices.  When you handle
the Drawable's you can get the world transform by either accumulating
the transform yourself during the NodeVisitor traversal or use
osg::Node::getWorldMatrices() or osg::computeLocalToWorld(NodePath)
using the NodeVisitor::getNodePath() for the node path.

Robert.
