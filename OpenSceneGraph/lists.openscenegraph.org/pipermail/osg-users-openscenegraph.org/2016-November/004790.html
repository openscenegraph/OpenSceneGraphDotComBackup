The purpose of providing support on the public osg-users mailing
list/forum is to help all users.  It provides a public record of
discussions so that others can learn or contribute to discussions.
Doing this public support costs us time and pulls us away from other
tasks, such as earning a wage so I for one want to make sure the
community get the widest benefit from it.

If users/companies want confidential/one or one support then the route
is consulting, training or via an annual support contract that
provides a confidential mailing list for your team.
