Hi guys,

Thanks for the replies.
I tried using the suggested ffmpeg plugin changes, but it didn't make a difference.
Turns out the culprit was automatic texture scaling to power of two texture size.
After setting the resizePowerOfTwoHint to false, I was able to play two 720p videos with 60fps.

Cheers,
Michael
