Hi Latha,

It's hard to work out exactly what you are trying to achieve and why.  The
.osg ascii format is a 3D model format, .tiff are for images+DEM's so
aren't directly compatible, you have to convert the .tiff into some form of
3d.  There are various ways of doing this but which way is appropriate is
not possible given how open to interpretation what you original wrote is.

Could you explain from a higher level what you are trying to achieve with
your application with the DEMs.
