Hi Brian,

I'm not sure what your definition of publicly maintained is but my company is providing Windows builds of OSG 3.4.0 at http://objexx.com/OpenSceneGraph.html. We set up an automated build system because we need these binaries ourselves and we found no other source for recent releases.

We are willing to entertain requests for additional 3rd party libraries and for builds of development releases. We will be posting 3.4.1 binaries within the next couple of weeks (waiting for Qt 5.9.2 or 5.10) and we could also post 3.4.0 builds with up-to-date 3rd party library versions.

If you have any questions about our builds just let me know.
