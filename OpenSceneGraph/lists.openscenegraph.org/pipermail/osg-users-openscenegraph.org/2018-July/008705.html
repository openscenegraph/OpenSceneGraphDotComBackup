Marlin,
This might work:

vec3 normalWorld = mat3(osg_ViewMatrixInverse) * gl_NormalMatrix *
gl_Normal;

But like Robert says, world coordinates on the GPU will lead to precision
loss, so only do it if you are content with a low-precision result.
