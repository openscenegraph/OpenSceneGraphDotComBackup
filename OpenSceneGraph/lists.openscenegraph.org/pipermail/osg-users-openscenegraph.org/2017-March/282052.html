Hi,

The matrices read from the 'rot' token appears to be read in wrong manner:

Here is an example line from AC3D file:

rot 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9

Inivis AC3D interprets that as:

1 4 7
2 5 8
3 6 9

While OpenSceneGraph reads it as:

1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9
