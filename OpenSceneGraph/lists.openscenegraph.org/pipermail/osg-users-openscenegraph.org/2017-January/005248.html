Hi Rambabu,
> Hi,Using Virtual Planet Builder(VPB) How to load builidings in osg ?

Virtual Planet Builder is for creation of paged terrain databases which 
has no functionality to place feature models AFAIK.

If you need more functionality in terms of geospecific/geotypical 
features to be placed, consider osgEarth.

In case you simply want to load buildings, osgDB::readNodeFile called 
with an appropriate building-model-file is your friend. ;-)

To summarize: Please stop shooting out questions without context. It is 
really not helping in getting solutions to your problems.
